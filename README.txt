== Description ==

Add the Clientity Chatbot window to your WordPress site.

Clientity is the automatic chatbot that enhances the customer support and user experience in your website.
Clientity also converts your visits into leads, by obtaining the visitor's contact information.

**Clientity is FREE** and assits your website visitors 24/7.

More info about Clientity at [clientity.com](https://clientity.com/)

Clientity works in **English** and **Spanish**.
[Contact us](https://clientity.com/#contact) to ask about your language.

--

Añade el Chatbot de Clientity a tu página web WordPress.

Clientity es el chatbot automático que mejora la atención al cliente en tu página web.
Además, Clientity convierte a tus visitantes en contactos, obteniendo su número de teléfono y su email.

**Clientity es GRATIS** y atiende tu página web las 24h.

Download the pulgin from your WordPress site, activate it, and get your [Bot coode at clientity.com](https://clientity.com/).

You should now see the chat window on your website.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I get a bot code? =

Sign up free at [clientity.com](https://clientity.com/). Then, sign in and go to **Integrations > WordPress** to see your bot code.


= ¿Dónde obtengo mi código de bot? =

Registrate gratis en [clientity.com](https://clientity.com/) y verás el código de bot en la sección **Integraciones > WordPress**.
